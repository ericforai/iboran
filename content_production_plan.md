# Content Production Plan: Energy Industry Digital Transformation

## Subject
**能源行业数智化：从“看得到”到“算得清”的进阶之路**
(Energy Digitalization: From "Visibility" to "Accountability")

## Target Reader
- **CIO/IT Director**: Concerned with technical architecture (iuap platform, AI integration).
- **Production Manager**: Focused on intelligent scheduling and safety.
- **CFO/Financial Director**: Focused on business-finance integration and cost control.

## Core Keywords
- 能源行业数智化
- 业财一体化
- 用友 BIP 超级版
- 智能化生产运作
- 绿色制造与碳足迹

## Key Takeaways (TL;DR)
1. 能源行业数智化的核心挑战：从单纯的监控（SCADA）向价值管理闭环转变。
2. 业财一体化的 3 个关键标志：实时对账、分批成本核算、预算在线控制。
3. 落地建议：避免一次性“大迁徙”，采用场景驱动的迭代模式。
